Hostel Management System (C#)

Escape Plan Game (C#)

School Management System (C++)

The console-based School Management System is designed to streamline the operations of a school by offering distinct roles for students and professors. Professor can add student, view and update their record, maintain their attendance and fee submission status, assign them marks, change their classes and their login password. Professor can also view subjects of all classes. Student has the access to view their class, subjects, marks, attendance, fee status, and result card. With an intuitive command-line interface, this system ensures efficient and user-friendly management of daily activities of an educational institute.
