About Education Projects
  • School Management System (C++)
    The console-based School Management System is designed to streamline the operations of a school by offering distinct roles for students and professors. Professor can add student, view and update their record, maintain their attendance and fee submission status, assign them marks, change their classes and their login password. Professor can also view subjects of all classes. Student has the access to view their class, subjects, marks, attendance, fee status, and result card. With an intuitive command-line interface, this system ensures efficient and user-friendly management of daily activities of an educational institute.

  • Space Shooter Game (C++)
    The console-based game "Space Shooter" is an exhilarating intergalactic adventure that challenges players to navigate through space while combating waves of enemy spacecraft. Players control a spaceship equipped with powerful weapons, engaging in intense battles against increasingly difficult adversaries. The game features a scoring system that rewards skillful maneuvering and accurate shooting, encouraging players to achieve high scores. With its retro-inspired graphics and fast-paced gameplay, Space Shooter provides an immersive and nostalgic experience, making it a compelling choice for fans of classic arcade-style games.
