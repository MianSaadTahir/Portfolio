About Me

As a 2nd-year Computer Science student at the University of Engineering and Technology Lahore, I am passionate about leveraging technology to solve complex problems and create innovative solutions.

With a strong foundation in Object-Oriented Programming, Database Systems, and Data Structures and Algorithms, I have honed my skills in C++, C#, Python, and SQL. I am also proficient in web development, utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to design and build interactive, user-friendly websites.

Through coursework and personal projects, my experience spans from developing desktop applications to creating full-fledged web projects, where I focus on clean code, scalability, and performance. I am always eager to learn new technologies and frameworks that enhance my ability to deliver impactful results.
